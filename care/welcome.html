After the exchange

Next steps — plain ones

If you entrusted the verdict to Jesus tonight, you do not need a program. You need a Person, a people, and a next honest act. Start here.

God, the record is true. I have sinned against You and I cannot pay the sentence. I believe Jesus Christ died in my place and rose. I turn from my sin — take it, and clothe me in His righteousness. From tonight, I am Yours.

1. Read — Gospel of John

One chapter a day. John was written “so that you may believe… and that by believing you may have life in his name” (John 20:31). When a line stops you, write one sentence: what this shows you about Jesus.

2. Belong — a Bible-teaching church

This was never meant to be walked alone. You do not need the perfect church. You need a real one — Scripture opened, Christ as Lord, ordinary people.

  • Scripture read and taught as if it has authority
  • Christ crucified and risen as the center, not a footnote
  • Ordinary people, not a performance only
  • Room to ask questions without being mocked

When you go, tell one person: “I’m new. I trusted Christ this week.” If gathering is impossible right now, still name a Christian who will pray with you by phone this week.

3. Speak — one living person

Tell a friend or family member: “I trusted Christ.” Confession is not a press release; it is how trust becomes public enough to be helped (Romans 10:9–10).

Need help finding a church?

We do not sell a franchise. Send your city (and country if outside the US). A person will look with you — unhurriedly — using the marks above. This is not an instant directory; it is a human reply.
